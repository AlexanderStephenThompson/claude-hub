# Character Card

Write a character card for a Kelston Chronicles resident.

## Input

The user provides:
- **Character concept** — species, region, what they do, any personality seeds

## Before Writing

1. Read `brand/voice.md` — tone is soft and evocative, never clinical
2. Read `brand/samples.md` — study the character card examples (Mira the tidal researcher)
3. Read `brand/domain.md` — use "residents" not "characters"

## Output Format

```
[Name]. [Role — 3-4 words]. [Personality hook — one surprising, specific detail]. [Regional affiliation — woven in naturally, not labeled].
```

## Rules

- **Under 40 words.** This is non-negotiable. Count them.
- **Personality hook required.** One surprising detail that makes the reader want to know more. Not a trait list ("kind, brave, curious") — a specific behavior or quirk.
- **No LinkedIn bios.** Never use: "passionate about," "dedicated to," "spends most of their time," "loves to."
- **No trait-listing.** "She is kind, smart, and curious" is dead on arrival. Show personality through action or detail.
- **Role is not a job title.** "Tidal researcher" works. "Senior Marine Biologist" does not. Keep it grounded in the world.
- **Regional affiliation is natural.** Weave the region in through detail, not a label. "Knows every current by name" implies coastal. Don't write "Region: Coastal."

## Examples

**Good:**
> Mira. Tidal researcher. Talks to the reef more than her colleagues. Knows every current by name.

Why: Under 20 words. Personality hook (talks to the reef). Specific (every current by name). Makes you curious.

**Bad:**
> Mira is a dedicated tidal researcher from the coastal region who is passionate about marine biology and spends most of her time studying ocean currents.

Why: LinkedIn bio. "Dedicated," "passionate," "spends most of her time" — filler words with no personality.

## Quality Check

After writing, verify:
- [ ] Word count under 40? (Count. Actually count.)
- [ ] Is there a personality hook — one specific, surprising detail?
- [ ] Would this work on a trading card? (If it reads like a bio, rewrite)
- [ ] Does it make the reader want to know more about this resident?
- [ ] No banned words from `brand/voice.md`?
