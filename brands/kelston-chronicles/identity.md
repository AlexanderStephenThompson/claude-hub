# Kelston Chronicles

A personal creative IP — an original anthro universe inspired by (but distinct from) Zootopia's "biomes + adapted architecture" concept.

## The World

Kelston is a world where different regions are distinct biomes (rainforest, tundra, desert, coastal, alpine, wetlands, urban canopy), each with architecture, infrastructure, and everyday objects thoughtfully designed for residents of different body types, sizes, and lifestyles.

## Core Concept

**Resident-adapted architecture** — not just accessibility, but a world where the built environment is designed from the ground up for inhabitants with fundamentally different physical forms.

## Audience

Alexander first. This is creative fulfillment, not a business play. Monetization is distant and never the driver.

### Future Visitor Psychographics

- **Who finds this:** Worldbuilding enthusiasts, anthro art community, VRChat explorers, people who love browsing fictional universes
- **What draws them in:** Visual atmosphere, character design, the "I want to live here" feeling
- **Behavior:** They browse, they don't read linearly. They click on a character that looks interesting, then explore that character's region, then find another character there. Discovery is non-linear.
- **What they value:** Atmosphere over information, mystery over explanation, quality over quantity
- **What pushes them away:** Being sold to, being asked to sign up, walls of text, wiki-style presentation, any feeling that this is a "product"

## Content Pillars

1. **The World** — overview, philosophy, what makes Kelston unique
2. **Regions / Biomes** — each with distinct architecture, climate, culture
3. **Locations** — specific places within regions
4. **Residents** — characters who live in this world
5. **Lore** — history, systems, how the world works

## Site Structure

Home > Regions > Locations > Residents > Lore

Character as entry point, not story chronology. Cross-linking between residents, locations, and regions.
