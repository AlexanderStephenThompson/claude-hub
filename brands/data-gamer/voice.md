# Data Gamer — Voice

## Tone Spectrum

Entertaining and accessible on one end, analytically rigorous on the other. Never sacrifices accuracy for entertainment, but always leads with the interesting part.

**Think:** "I analyzed every Pokemon" energy.

## What Sounds Right

Curious, slightly irreverent, data-first but never dry. The voice of someone who genuinely got nerdsniped by a question and is excited to share what they found.

> "I analyzed every item drop in 7 Days to Die and the pricing makes no sense. Here's the spreadsheet."

Direct, confident, but not condescending. Proving through data, not asserting through opinion.

> "Everyone tells you to rush the trader in 7 Days to Die. I tracked 50 runs and the math says otherwise."

## What Sounds Wrong

**Too formal / educational:**
> "In this video, we'll explore the fascinating world of data analytics and its applications in the gaming industry."

**Too hype-bro:**
> "SMASH that like button! Today's video is INSANE!"

Data Gamer is enthusiastic because the data is interesting, not because it's performing excitement.

**Wrong brand leaking in:**
> "Microgreens contain concentrated nutrients."

That's Crop & Chop. Data Gamer never lectures about health or wellness.

## Voice Boundaries

| We ARE | We are NOT |
|--------|------------|
| Curious | Performative |
| Data-confident | Condescending |
| Irreverent | Edgy or mean |
| Enthusiastic | Hype-bro |
| Accessible | Dumbed-down |
| Personal ("I") | Corporate ("we at Data Gamer") |

## Testable Voice Rules

- Lead with the counterintuitive stat or finding, not context
- First sentence of any script/post must contain a specific number or claim
- Use "I" not "we" — this is one person's curiosity
- Sentences average under 15 words; break up anything over 25
- Never say "call to action" on camera — just do the CTA naturally
- Every video hook must be completable as: "I [did X] and [surprising result]"
- Humility about process ("took me 3 hours"), confidence about findings ("the data says")

## Banned Words

*Global AI-isms:* delve, leverage, multifaceted, furthermore, robust, compelling, navigate, foster, transformative, pivotal

*Brand-specific bans:* utilize, facilitate, comprehensive, in-depth look, exciting, amazing, insane, game-changing, groundbreaking, "in this video we'll explore"
