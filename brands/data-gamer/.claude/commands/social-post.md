# /social-post

Write a social media post promoting a video or sharing a finding.

## Rules

- Under 30 words
- Lead with the surprising finding, not "check out my new video"
- Gaming-first framing — the game is the hook
- No hype-bro energy — no "INSANE," "amazing," "exciting"
- No "excited to announce" or generic promo language
- Specific numbers required
- Use "I" not "we"
