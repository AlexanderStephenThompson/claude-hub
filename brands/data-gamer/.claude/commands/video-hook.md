# /video-hook

Generate 5 hook options for a Data Gamer YouTube video.

## Rules

- Each hook must follow the pattern: "I [did X] and [surprising result]"
- Challenge a common belief or assumption gamers hold
- First sentence must contain a specific number or claim
- No "In this video, we'll explore..." — ever
- No describing what the video covers — make the viewer curious
- Gaming-first framing — the game is the subject, data is the proof

## Output

5 hook options, each 1-2 sentences. Ranked by how counterintuitive the claim is.
