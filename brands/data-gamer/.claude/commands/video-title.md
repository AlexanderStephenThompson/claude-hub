# /video-title

Generate 5 YouTube title options.

## Rules

- Lead with the gaming claim, not data framing
- Include specific numbers ("Every," "200," "50 runs")
- Bold claims that make you click to verify
- No "Data-Driven Guide," "Comprehensive Analysis," or educational framing
- Think about what gamers actually search for — "is the trader worth it" not "trader analysis"
- Titles should work as both a promise and a challenge

## Output

5 title options ranked by click appeal. Each under 60 characters.
