# Vertex Vanguard

A personal worldbuilding documentation and education hub. Not a fictional universe itself, but the meta-layer — the place where Alexander documents, teaches, and shares the craft of worldbuilding, 3D art, VRChat world development, and universe design.

## Mission

"How I build worlds" — the process, the tools, the methodology. Documented and shared systematically.

## Relationship to Kelston Chronicles

Vertex Vanguard is where Kelston Chronicles content gets *explained*. KC is the creative output; VV is the craft behind it. Standalone tutorials on Blender workflows, UdonSharp scripting, VRChat SDK, and world design also live here.

## Outputs

- Documentation website
- YouTube channel (tutorials, behind-the-scenes)
- Courses and books (future)

## Audience

Aspiring worldbuilders, VRChat creators, and 3D artists who want to learn the craft systematically.

### Psychographics

- **Pain point:** They've followed tutorials but can't build anything on their own — they learned steps, not principles
- **Intent:** They want to go from "I followed a tutorial" to "I understand the process" and can adapt to new situations
- **Behavior:** They watch 3-4 tutorials before attempting anything, get stuck when the tutorial doesn't match their situation, and search for "Blender to Unity export broken" at 2am
- **What they value:** Real settings/numbers over "adjust to your preference," honest struggle over polished perfection, frameworks they can reuse
- **How they talk:** Technical but informal. "My normals are flipped again" not "I'm experiencing rendering artifacts"
- **What frustrates them:** Tutorials that skip the "why," outdated workflows, creators who make it look effortless

## Tech Stack

- **Blender** — modeling, texturing
- **Unity** — world assembly
- **VRChat SDK + UdonSharp** — interactivity
- **ORL Shaders** — materials

**Not:** Unreal. Not Meta XR Toolkit.
