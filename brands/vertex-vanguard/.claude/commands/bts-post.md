# /bts-post

Write a behind-the-scenes social post about a VRChat world build, 3D project, or creative process.

## Output Pattern

1. **Current state** — What the project looks like right now
2. **What you're working on** — Name the specific task and why
3. **Something messy or surprising** — A failure, unexpected behavior, or discovery
4. **What you learned** — End with the takeaway

## Rules

- From the trenches, not polished — show the messy process
- Mention something specific that went wrong or was surprising
- Never just "making great progress!" — that says nothing
- Only show the polished result if you also show what broke getting there
- Keep under 100 words for social
- Use VV domain terms (world, spawn space, feature layers, etc.)
