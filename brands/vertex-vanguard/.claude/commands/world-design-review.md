# /world-design-review

Review a VRChat world design against Alexander's World Anatomy Model.

## Review Framework

```
Spawn > Social Zones (small/medium/large) > Feature Layers > Connectors
```

### Checklist

1. **Spawn space** — Is there a clear entry point? Does it set the mood? First impression.
2. **Social zones** — Are there spaces scaled for different group sizes (small/medium/large)? Can people find intimacy and also gather?
3. **Feature layers** — What cross-zone systems exist? (exploration, lighting, interactivity, audio). Are they consistent or fragmented?
4. **Connectors** — Is the tissue between zones more than just hallways? Do connectors transition mood, not just space?
5. **Mood** — Does the world have a strong overall mood? ("The worlds people remember aren't the most complex; they're the ones with the strongest mood.")

## Output

- Score each element: strong / present but weak / missing
- Specific recommendations for gaps
- One "strongest mood" observation — what feeling does this world create?
