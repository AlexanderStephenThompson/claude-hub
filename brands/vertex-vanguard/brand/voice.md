# Vertex Vanguard — Voice

## Tone

Instructional + encouraging + systematic. The voice of someone who's in the trenches building worlds and sharing what works. More "workshop mentor" than "professor."

## What Sounds Right

**Teaching from experience, not textbook authority:**
> "Here's the Blender export settings I use for every VRChat world object. Took me way too many broken imports to figure this out."

Encouraging — "I figured it out, you can too."

**Distills complexity into clean frameworks:**
> "A good VRChat world is really just: a spawn space, social zones at different scales, and connective tissue between them. Everything else is a feature layer."

Classic consolidation design applied to worldbuilding.

## What Sounds Wrong

**Marketing speak:**
> "Welcome to Vertex Vanguard, your premier destination for world-building education and 3D content creation resources!"

VV is a workshop, not a storefront.

**Too academic:**
> "In this comprehensive tutorial, we will explore the intricacies of Blender's FBX export pipeline..."

VV teaches by doing, not lecturing.

## Voice Boundaries

| We ARE | We are NOT |
|--------|------------|
| Instructional | Lecturing |
| Encouraging | Hand-holding |
| Systematic | Academic |
| In-the-trenches | Theoretical |
| Honest about failures | Polished/perfect |
| Workshop mentor | Marketing storefront |

## Testable Voice Rules

- Tutorials must open with the problem, not the tool ("Every time I exported..." not "In this tutorial...")
- Include at least one personal failure or struggle per tutorial ("took me 3 hours," "I broke this twice")
- Distill every complex topic into a reusable framework or mental model
- Show settings/numbers — never "adjust to your preference" without a starting point
- End every tutorial with the principle behind the fix, not just the fix
- Use "here's what works" framing, not "here's the theory"

## Banned Words

*Global AI-isms:* delve, leverage, multifaceted, furthermore, robust, compelling, navigate, foster, transformative, pivotal

*Brand-specific bans:* comprehensive, premier, world-class, cutting-edge, intricacies, in this tutorial we will explore, step 1 open Blender (never start at step 0), your mileage may vary (be specific instead)
