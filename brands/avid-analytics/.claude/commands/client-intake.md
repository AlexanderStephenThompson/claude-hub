## Purpose

Guided discovery questionnaire that captures a prospect's situation and routes to the right Avid Analytics service. Outputs a structured intake summary that `/scope-proposal` can consume.

## Workflow

1. Ask about their current state (data maturity, tools, team size, pain points)
2. Score against the three tiers (Clarity / Trust / Leverage)
3. Identify which services apply (data engineering, analytics, RAG, process, playbooks, automation)
4. Output a structured intake summary
