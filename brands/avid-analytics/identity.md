# Avid Analytics

A productized data and process consulting business serving small and mid-sized teams. Helps organizations go from scattered, messy operations to clean workflows and trusted data through fixed-scope service packages.

## Mission

Human sanity today, AI readiness tomorrow.

## Three-Tier Service Model

Each tier builds on the previous:

| Tier | Name | Focus | Transformation |
|------|------|-------|---------------|
| 1 | **Clarity** | Unstructured data — files, folders, shared drives | Organized and navigable |
| 2 | **Trust** | Structured data — databases, pipelines, integrations | Reliable and documented |
| 3 | **Leverage** | Activated data — AI readiness, RAG, automation | Future-proofed |

## Positioning

"I help small and mid-sized teams go from scattered ideas and messy processes to a working MVP and clean workflows by offering fixed-scope data, process, and design packages, so they can move faster with less friction and guesswork."

## Audience

Small and mid-sized teams (5-50 people) who know their data is a mess but don't know where to start.

### Psychographics

- **Pain point:** They waste hours finding files, don't trust their reports, and know they're leaving value on the table — but hiring a full-time data person isn't feasible
- **Intent:** They want someone to come in, fix it, and leave them with a system they can maintain
- **Behavior:** They've probably tried to organize things themselves 2-3 times and it didn't stick. They Google "how to organize shared drive" at 11pm.
- **What they value:** Clarity over cleverness, defined scope over open-ended engagements, someone who explains the "why" not just the "what"
- **How they talk:** Non-technical. "Our files are a mess" not "our information architecture needs restructuring"
- **What they fear:** Scope creep, being sold more than they need, consultants who make things more complicated
