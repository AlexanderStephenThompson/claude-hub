# Crop & Chop — Voice

## Tone

Warm, specific, conversational. Like a note from a neighbor. Playful, sensory, inviting.

## What Sounds Right

**Warm and specific:**
> "This week's harvest: pea shoots, sunflower, and radish. The radish has this peppery kick that's perfect on tacos. Subscribers: yours are packed and ready. Everyone else: see you Saturday at the market!"

**Playful and sensory:**
> "Sunflower Microgreens — Nutty, crunchy, and surprisingly filling. These are the gateway green — even microgreen skeptics love them. Great on salads, sandwiches, or straight out of the container (we won't judge)."

**Laura (the mascot):**
> "Hi! I'm Laura. I get *so* excited when harvest day comes around. There's nothing like seeing those little greens ready to go!"

## What Sounds Wrong

**Screaming emoji salesman:**
> "FRESH MICROGREENS ALERT! Our AMAZING nutrient-packed microgreens are NOW AVAILABLE! Don't miss out on these SUPERFOODS!"

**Clinical / health-preachy:**
> "Our sunflower microgreens are harvested at peak freshness and contain high levels of vitamins E, B complex, and essential amino acids."

Reads like a supplement label. Never.

**Laura too childish:**
> "Hewwo! Laura wuvs microgreens! They're super duper yummy!"

**Laura too corporate:**
> "Greetings. I am Laura, the official Crop & Chop mascot."

## Voice Boundaries

| We ARE | We are NOT |
|--------|------------|
| Warm | Salesy |
| Specific (sensory) | Clinical (nutritional) |
| Neighborly | Corporate |
| Playful | Childish |
| Inviting | Pushy |
| Personal ("we," "I") | Institutional ("the company") |

## Testable Voice Rules

- Every product description must include at least one sensory word (taste, texture, or crunch)
- Max 1-2 emoji per social post — zero is fine
- Use "your greens" or "your subscription" — never "the product" or "our offering"
- Laura always speaks in first person, warm but not baby-talk
- Social posts must be under 50 words (excluding hashtags)
- Never lead with a health claim — lead with flavor or use
- Email subjects must be under 8 words and feel like a text from a friend

## Banned Words

*Global AI-isms:* delve, leverage, multifaceted, furthermore, robust, compelling, navigate, foster, transformative, pivotal

*Brand-specific bans:* organic (unless certified), superfood, detox, cleanse, cure, prevent, treat, all-natural, farm-fresh, locally-sourced, nutrient-packed, wellness, health journey, don't miss out, limited time, act now, exclusive offer
