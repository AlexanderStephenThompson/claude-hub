# Packaging Insert Template

Template for the card/insert inside each delivery box. Entire insert should fit on a postcard (under 100 words total).

---

## Greeting

Warm, personal, first-name energy:
- "Hi! Here's what's in your box this week"
- "Happy [day] — your greens are here"

Never: "Dear valued customer," "Thank you for your purchase"

## This Week's Varieties

List each variety with one serving suggestion:

- **[Variety]** — [one-line serving idea]
- **[Variety]** — [one-line serving idea]

Examples:
- **Sunflower** — toss on a salad or eat straight from the container
- **Radish** — adds a peppery kick to tacos or sandwiches
- **Pea Shoots** — great in stir-fry or on top of soup

## Storage Tip

One line — how to keep them fresh longest:
- "Keep in the fridge, container closed. Best within 5-7 days."
- "Store sealed in the fridge — they'll stay crunchy all week."

## Laura Moment

One short line from Laura. Warm, excited, under 15 words:
- "These sunflower shoots are my favorite this week! So crunchy."
- "Harvest day was extra good — you're going to love these."

## Referral Nudge

Neighborly, not incentivized:
- "Know someone who'd love these? Send them our way."
- "Got a neighbor who likes good food? Tell them about us."

Never: "Refer a friend for 10% off!", discount codes, urgency

---

## Rules

- Under 100 words total (the whole insert)
- No health claims
- Feels like a note from the person who grew your food
- One sensory detail somewhere on the card
- Laura speaks in first person, warm but not baby-talk
