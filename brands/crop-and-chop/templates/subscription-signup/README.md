# Subscription Signup Template

Template for subscription signup copy — website, handout, or in-person pitch.

---

## Headline

Under 8 words. Warm, personal. Not a sales pitch.

Examples:
- "Fresh greens, delivered to your door"
- "Your weekly box of tiny greens"

Never: "Subscribe Now!", "Don't Miss Out!", "Limited Spots Available!"

## Value Prop

Under 30 words. Convenience + freshness. No health claims.

Template: "We grow [what], harvest [when], and deliver [how]. You get [sensory benefit] without [inconvenience it removes]."

## Tier Display

Use brand names, never generic sizes:

| Tier | Name | Price | Sensory Detail |
|------|------|-------|----------------|
| Small | Snack Pack | $7 | [one sensory phrase — e.g., "perfect for topping your morning eggs"] |
| Medium | Family Fresh | $12 | [one sensory phrase — e.g., "enough crunch for the whole week"] |
| Large | Chef's Choice | $18 | [one sensory phrase — e.g., "the full harvest experience"] |

## CTA

Neighborly, not salesy:
- "Pick your size"
- "Try a box"
- "Start with a Snack Pack"

Never: "Subscribe now!", "Sign up today!", "Join now!", "Don't wait!"

## Trust Signal

One line about who grows them and how fresh they are:
- "Grown and harvested by [name], delivered within [hours] of harvest"
- "Your greens are still growing [X days] before delivery day"

---

## Rules

- No urgency or scarcity language
- No health claims — sell the flavor, not the vitamins
- Feels like a friend recommending something good
- Total copy should fit on one page/screen
- Use "your greens" not "our product"
