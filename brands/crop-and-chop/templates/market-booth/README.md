# Market Booth Template

Template for farmers market booth signage and display copy. Everything must be readable at a glance — no paragraphs on signage.

---

## Banner

Use the tagline exactly as written: **"Tiny greens, big flavor"**

No modifications. No additions. This is the banner text.

## Variety Cards

One card per variety on display. Each card:

- **Variety name** (large, readable from 3 feet)
- **One sensory phrase** (under 8 words — taste or texture, not health)

Examples:
- "Sunflower — Nutty, crunchy, surprisingly filling"
- "Radish — Peppery kick, perfect on tacos"
- "Pea Shoots — Sweet and crisp, kids love them"

Never: nutrient lists, health claims, serving sizes

## Price Display

Clean and simple. Tier names and prices only:

```
Snack Pack     $7
Family Fresh   $12
Chef's Choice  $18
```

No "starting at," no asterisks, no fine print.

## Sample Invitation

"Try one" energy. Short, warm:
- "Want to try one?"
- "Grab a sample"
- "See which one's your favorite"

Never: "FREE SAMPLES!", "Try before you buy!", anything in all caps

## Subscription Teaser

One line, points to signup. On a small card or table tent:
- "Love these? We deliver. Ask us about subscriptions."
- "Get these delivered every week — ask how"

---

## Rules

- Max 10 words per sign element
- Every variety card has a sensory word
- No health claims on any booth material
- No urgency language
- Readable at a glance — if someone walking past can't get it in 2 seconds, it's too long
