# Crop & Chop

A local microgreens subscription business — Alexander's primary cash flow engine. Sells fresh microgreens at farmers markets and through subscriptions.

## Mission

Be the friendly neighbor who figured out how to grow incredible microgreens and wants to share.

## Audience

Local customers in the farmers market radius — health-conscious but not health-obsessed.

### Psychographics

- **Pain point:** Grocery store greens are sad, wilted, and tasteless. They want something genuinely fresh but don't have time to grow their own.
- **Intent:** They're at the market already — they want to try something new, support a local grower, and eat better without overthinking it
- **Behavior:** They buy with their eyes and their taste buds. They'll try a free sample before they buy. They subscribe if the first order impresses them.
- **What they value:** Freshness they can taste (not just claimed), knowing who grew it, convenience (subscription = one less thing to think about)
- **How they talk:** "These are really good" not "I appreciate the nutrient density." Casual, sensory, neighborhood-level.
- **What they don't want:** To be lectured about health, to feel like they're buying from a wellness brand, to deal with complicated ordering

## Revenue Goal

Stable local cash flow to fund everything else.
