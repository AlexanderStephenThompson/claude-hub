# /market-email

Write an email to Crop & Chop subscribers.

## Rules

- **Subject line:** Under 8 words, feels like a text from a friend. No title case, no urgency.
- **Body:** Mentions sensory details (taste, texture), addresses subscribers warmly
- No urgency language — no "don't miss out," "limited supply," "act now"
- No health claims — lead with flavor or use
- Use "your greens" or "your subscription" — never "the product"
- Keep body under 100 words

## Pattern

1. Subject line (personal, short)
2. What's in this week's harvest (specific varieties)
3. One sensory highlight
4. Pickup/delivery details
5. Warm close
