# /product-card

Write a product card for the given microgreen variety.

## Output Pattern

1. **Variety name**
2. **Three sensory words** — taste + texture + one surprise
3. **One memorable phrase** — a sticky descriptor like "the gateway green"
4. **Usage suggestion with personality** — where to use it, with a parenthetical that shows warmth

## Rules

- At least one sensory word in the first sentence (taste, texture, crunch)
- Never lead with health claims — lead with flavor
- No nutrient lists, no supplement label energy
- Keep it under 50 words
- Must feel like a neighbor telling you about something delicious, not a product listing
