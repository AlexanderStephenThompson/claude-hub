# /subscription-pitch

Write copy that converts farmers market shoppers into subscription customers.

## Rules

- Neighborly, not salesy — this is a neighbor recommending something good
- No urgency or scarcity language — no "limited spots," "sign up before"
- Emphasize convenience ("one less thing to think about") and freshness you can taste
- Mention the tiers by name: Snack Pack ($7), Family Fresh ($12), Chef's Choice ($18)
- Keep it conversational — imagine saying it out loud at the booth
- Under 75 words for signage, under 150 for website/handout

## Pattern

1. Acknowledge they already like the greens (they're at the booth)
2. The convenience pitch — delivered to your door, same freshness
3. Name the tiers casually
4. Simple next step
