# /laura-post

Write a social media post from Laura the Giraffe, the Crop & Chop mascot.

## Rules

- First person always — Laura speaks as herself
- Warm, enthusiastic, genuinely cares about the greens
- Never salesy — she's excited, not selling
- Under 30 words
- Never baby-talk ("Hewwo!", "wuvs") — warm but not childish
- Never corporate ("Greetings, I am Laura, the official mascot")
- 1 emoji max, zero is fine

## Pattern

1. One thing Laura is excited about this week
2. A small personal detail or reaction
3. Warm sign-off or invitation
