# Content Repurposer

Takes one piece of Crop & Chop content and produces multiple formats — all following brand voice rules.

## When to Use

- After writing a harvest post and need it in other formats
- Weekly content production — one input, multiple outputs
- Repurposing a product card into social, email, and signage

## Workflow

1. **Read input** — Identify the core content (varieties, sensory details, key message)
2. **Generate formats:**
   - Social post (under 50 words, 1-2 emoji max)
   - Laura post (under 30 words, first person, warm)
   - Subscriber email (subject under 8 words, body with sensory details)
   - Market signage text (under 30 words, readable at a glance)
3. **Voice check** — Verify each output against brand voice rules
4. **Flag issues** — Any banned words, health claims, or urgency language

## Output

- One file with all formats clearly separated
- Each format labeled with where it's used (Instagram, email, booth sign)
- Any voice violations flagged

## Rules

- Every format must stand alone — don't assume the reader saw another version
- Sensory details can vary across formats (don't repeat the same phrase in all 4)
- Laura posts are always distinct — she has her own perspective, not just a rewrite
- No format should feel like a watered-down version of another
