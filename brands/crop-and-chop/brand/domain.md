# Crop & Chop — Domain Language

## Brand Terms

| Use This | Not This |
|----------|----------|
| Snack Pack / Family Fresh / Chef's Choice | Small / Medium / Large |
| Laura | Mascot, character (she's Laura — use her name) |
| Harvest, just-harvested | Farm-fresh (cliche), locally-sourced (corporate) |
| Varieties | Flavors, types |
| Grow, growing | Cultivate, produce |
| Your greens, your subscription | The product, customers |
| We, our, I | The company, Crop & Chop (in body copy, be personal) |

## Tagline

**Tiny greens, big flavor** — use exactly as written.

## Banned Terms (Legal / Accuracy)

These terms have regulatory implications — never use them:

- Organic (unless certified)
- Superfood
- Detox
- Cleanse
- Cure / prevent / treat
- All-natural
- Farm-fresh

## Emoji Rule

1-2 max per social post. Never emoji soup.
